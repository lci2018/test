Responsive vs Adaptive Design

You have two techniques:

  1. Responsive Web Design (RWD): Same layout will automatically respond to that size, like a single ball growing or shrinking to fit through several different hoops.
  2. Adaptive Web Design: The layout used depends on the screen size used. You’d have several different balls to use depending on the hoop size.

Responsive vs Adaptative Design

You have two approaches:

  1. Graceful degradation: Design for large screens (like desktop) and remove items and functionalities when goes to small screens. PLEASE DON'T USE THAT.
  2. Progressive enhacement: Design for small screens and add functionalities when goes to large screens. THAT'S THE WAY (and I like it)
